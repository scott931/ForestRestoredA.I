What we Do

Forest Restored A.I is a Kenyan-based startup that uses AI as an early warning system to detect deforestation, wildfires and charcoal burning

Imagine catching deforestation before a tree is cut or extinguishing wildfires before they spread. Thats what we do.

Using AI, we reduce the time, energy, and effort it takes to detect environmental threats such as deforestation and wildfires by detecting them at onset. 

And our AI doesn’t just detect problems; we can also predict some of these challenges before they happen.

This way, we protect biodiversity from loss and create efficiency in environmental monitoring. Tech for Good in action! 💚

Precision Detection 🔍

Advanced AI identifies threats at their inception.

Real-Time Alerts 🚨

Notifications sent directly to response teams.

Sustainable Impact 🌱

Proven to reduce logging in hotspots like Ngong Forest, Kenya.

Doris Damaris

Founder & CEO, Forest Restored A.I

Doris Damaris Njagi is a visionary entrepreneur driving the intersection of climate action, sustainable design, and inclusive economic development in Africa. With a strong foundation in climate finance and banking, Doris has spent years shaping financial strategies that support green innovation and environmentally resilient businesses. Her experience in the financial sector — including structuring climate‑aligned lending products and integrating ESG frameworks within commercial banking — gives her a unique ability to link capital, impact, and community growth.

Doris founded Forest Restored A.I to tackle urgent environmental challenges such as mangrove deforestation while creating dignified livelihoods for women across Kenya's coastal, urban, and rural communities. Under her leadership, she has empowered thousands of women to generate sustainable income by transforming deforestation to thriving forest land and advancing large‑scale forest restoration with data‑driven monitoring tools.

A forward‑thinking leader, Doris is supported by a dedicated team of technologists and AI specialists, enabling Forest Restored A.I to pioneer the use of AI and IoT for ecosystem monitoring, supply‑chain efficiency, and climate‑resilient solutions. Together, they are building Africa's next generation of climate‑positive enterprises — combining local creativity with cutting‑edge technology to protect nature and uplift communities.

How We Do It

How Forest Restored A.I Works ⚙️

Our goal is to ensure that no forest threat goes unnoticed.

Using machine learning algorithms, Forest Restored A.I assesses risks, validates events, and mobilizes action teams to prevent disasters.

Aside from tech, we also engage local grassroot communities on the importance of biodiversity protection.

Watch Our Explainer Video

1. Detection: Onground sensors in the forest identify threats

2. Analysis: Our machine learning models process and assess the threat

3. Notification: Emergency alerts are generated and sent out

4. Action: Response teams/authorities take relevant immediate steps
